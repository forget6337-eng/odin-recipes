Classic Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

Meat sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • Salt and pepper

Béchamel sauce:

  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g flour
  • 500 ml milk
  • Salt and nutmeg

Other:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Grated cheese
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil, cook onion, add garlic, then brown the beef.
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer 15–20 minutes.
  3. Melt butter, add flour, then whisk in milk. Cook until thick and smooth.
  4. Layer sauce, pasta sheets, and béchamel. Repeat and finish with cheese.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 35–40 minutes. Let rest before serving.

Tips

  • Add red wine for extra flavor.
  • Let it rest before cutting.
  • Try vegetarian alternatives.
