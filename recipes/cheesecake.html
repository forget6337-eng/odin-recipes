Cheesecake

Classic Cheesecake

Ingredients

For the base:

  • 200 g digestive biscuits
  • 100 g melted butter

For the filling:

  • 600 g cream cheese
  • 150 g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 ml cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Crush the biscuits into fine crumbs and mix with melted butter.
  2. Press the mixture into the bottom of a baking pan to form the base.
  3. In a bowl, mix cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in cream and vanilla.
  5. Pour the filling over the base.
  6. Bake at 160°C (320°F) for 45–50 minutes.
  7. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips

  • Do not overmix to avoid cracks.
  • Let it cool slowly for best texture.
  • Top with fruit, chocolate, or caramel.
